Swagatam Krishna

Saadhana 2025’s performance concluded with a presentation of Swagatam Krishna, where the devotee is saying O Krishna, please come, let me touch your feet.

The piece depicts various stories from Krishna’s life, including the story of Kaliya and the Gopis.

Performed by Amaira Nayyar, Anandita Sulakshana Arya, Ishika Pant, Jaysha Tandon, Mananya Thakur, Noor Malik, Saanvi Surya, Shoumili Monika Sen and Sirisha Agnihotri.
